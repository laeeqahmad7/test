Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rish scramled eggs.

Ingradients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter ina frying pan over a medium beat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are macking an omelette)
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
